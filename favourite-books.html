Digital Minimalism

Cal Newport


A guide into the forces that shape the internet, mainly the Digital Attention Economy. Also contains helpful tips about dealing with information overload and economizing tech usage.

How Not To Die

Dr. Michael Greger


An evidence-based argument about the role that diet and lifestyle play in the development, reversal, or prevention of all-cause mortality and chronic disease. Think prescribing food as medicine to prevent heart disease, brain disease, cancers, type-2 diabetes, depression, and anxiety.
